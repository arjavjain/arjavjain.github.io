About Animals

There are about 10,000 species of animals in the world and 950,000 species of insects.There are 3 million animals on earth and still counting.They are from kingdom animalia (also called metazoa). All the animals are heterotrophs.The animals are divided into: KINGDOM - PHYLUM - CLASS - ORDER - FAMILY - GENUS - SPECIES

Charles Darwin's Theory of Evolution increased the curiosity on animals .Before it,people thought that animals remain same throughout there life but Theory of Evolution says that animals evolve gradually from common ancestors.Some species survived through a process called "natural selection, "where species that successfully adapted to meet the changing requirements of their natural habitat thrived , while those that failed to evolve and reproduce died off.This Theory of Evolution
